Wedding Venue Booking App

The wedding venue booking application is a software designed to make the process of listing and booking wedding venues quick and simple for users.

The aim of the project was to create a mobile application for booking wedding venues that would allow venue owners list their available venues for booking and let users book venues for a wedding celebration.

The application was created with modern software development techniques and tools, such as agile methodology, flutter, dart, firebase, and was tested rigorously to ensure its quality.

Several testing techniques, such as widget testing, integration testing, user acceptance testing, and usability testing, were used throughout the development process. The outcomes of these tests showed that the app was of a high quality and met with the requirements and specifications established during the project's earlier stages.
